Technical
Specifications

Structure & Walls

R.C.C. framed structure with Sarvotham/Jairaj Steel. 9" External & 4.5" Internal walls with high-quality bricks.

🚪

Teak Wood Joinery

Indian Teak Wood frames. Main door with Teak Wood shutters (BT). UPVC windows with safety grills.

🛁

Plumbing & Sanitary

CERA make WC and Taps. Concealed piping for geysers with 7' high glazed tile dado in toilets.

Electricals

Nakoda Company wiring with Anchor Penta switches. Dedicated power points for appliances.

Flooring & Painting

24" x 24" Vitrified tiles. Birla Wall Putty interior with ACE paint for external walls.

🏬

Amenities

6-Passenger lift. Bore water supply with motor and Manjeera water connection.
